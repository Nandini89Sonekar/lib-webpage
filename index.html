About us

The university has a well-stocked library. The library has a collection of over 1000 books that students make use of to excel in their field of study. The Library users have been able to enlighten themselves by going through the newly added books.

Discover a welcoming and supportive study environment in our library. Explore our open stacks, utilize the web center for research, or find a quiet corner in our spacious reading room. Our friendly and experienced library staff are always ready to assist you with finding the right resources and navigating our facilities. We invite you to become a regular and engaged member of our library community.
